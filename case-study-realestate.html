CASE STUDY

Real Estate Firm Cuts Onboarding from 3 Days to 5 Minutes

Automated document collection, e-signature workflows, and CRM updates eliminate 15+ hours of manual paperwork per week.

THE CHALLENGE

The Manual Bottleneck

The Situation

A mid-sized real estate firm with 10+ agents was spending days on each client onboarding:

  • ✗ Clients manually fill out forms (ID verification, address, financing)
  • ✗ Email back & forth for document collection (can take days)
  • ✗ Manual document review & verification
  • ✗ Client signing required (DocuSign process took another day+)
  • ✗ Manual data entry into Salesforce (errors, duplicate records)

The Business Impact

  • 15+ hours per week wasted on paperwork and admin
  • 3-5 days average onboarding time (poor client experience)
  • Data errors and duplicates in CRM causing confusion
  • Paralegal role tied up doing manual data entry
THE SOLUTION

Instant Client Onboarding Flow

The Automated Workflow

1

Smart Form Collection

Single link sent to client with intelligent form (adaptive based on responses)

2

Automatic Document Request

Based on form responses, automated emails request specific docs (ID, proof of funds, etc.)

3

DocuSign Integration

Required agreements auto-populated with client data → sent for e-signature

4

Salesforce Auto-Update

All data auto-enters Salesforce with proper fields mapped → zero duplicate records

5

Team Notification

Slack notification alerts agents immediately when client is onboarded and ready

6

Instant Client Welcome

Automated welcome email with all next steps and document checklist

Tech Stack

  • Salesforce - CRM
  • DocuSign - e-Signature
  • n8n - Custom automation
  • Slack - Notifications

Timeline

  • Week 1: Document current process & requirements
  • Week 2: Build n8n workflows & Salesforce mappings
  • Week 3: Test with sample clients, refine
  • Week 4: Live launch & team training
RESULTS

Transformation in Minutes

5 min

Onboarding Time

Down from 3-5 days. Clients complete entire process before end of call.

15 hrs

Weekly Time Saved

Paralegal freed up for higher-value work instead of data entry.

100%

Data Accuracy

Zero duplicate records. Zero manual entry errors.

$35K

Annual Savings

Paralegal time no longer spent on manual tasks.

Business Impact

Their $10,000 investment was recovered in ~3 months. Better client experience = more referrals.

5x ROI Year 1
