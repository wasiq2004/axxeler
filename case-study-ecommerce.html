CASE STUDY

Global E-Commerce Brand Cuts Order Processing Time by 95%

How we automated their entire Shopify to 3PL fulfillment process, saving 40 hours per week and eliminating shipping errors completely.

THE CHALLENGE

The Breaking Point

The Situation

This e-commerce company was processing 500+ orders daily across Shopify. But their team was manually:

  • ✗ Logging into Shopify to check orders
  • ✗ Copying customer data to Excel
  • ✗ Uploading to their 3PL fulfillment system
  • ✗ Sending tracking info back to customers
  • ✗ Managing refunds and order cancellations

The Impact

  • 40+ hours/week wasted on manual data entry
  • 1-2% shipping error rate costing them customer relationships
  • Team burnout from repetitive work
  • Slow customer experience - delays in shipping notifications
THE SOLUTION

End-to-End Order Automation

The Automated Workflow We Built

1

Shopify Order Trigger

When new order is placed, webhook triggers instantly

2

Extract & Enrich Order Data

Zapier pulls customer info, adds shipping method, applies business rules

3

3PL Integration

Order automatically uploaded to their fulfillment partner API

4

Inventory Sync

Stock levels updated in Shopify in real-time (prevents overselling)

5

Automatic Customer Notification

Customer gets instant email with tracking number (zero delays)

6

Exception Handling

Failed shipments auto-flagged → Slack alert → manual review if needed

Tech Stack

  • Shopify - E-commerce platform
  • Zapier - Workflow automation
  • ShipStation - 3PL management
  • Slack - Team notifications

Timeline

  • Week 1: Audit & mapping workflow
  • Week 2: Build & test integration
  • Week 3: Pilot with 20% orders
  • Week 4: Full rollout & training

KEY LEARNINGS

What Made This Successful

1. Start With Pilot Phase

We ran the automation on 20% of orders for a week to test edge cases. This identified and fixed issues before full rollout.

2. Exception Handling is Critical

Real-world edge cases (invalid addresses, inventory mismatches) needed manual escalation. We built these safety nets in.

3. Team Buy-In Matters

Training the team on how to monitor and handle alerts prevented panic when the system went live.

4. Logging & Monitoring

Every transaction is logged. The team can see exactly what happened to each order without digging into databases.

5. Scalability Built In

System handles 10x order volume without changes. Future-proof from day one.

6. Continuous Optimization

Monthly check-ins led to new automations (e.g., automated refund processing). System improves over time.
