CASE STUDY

B2B Digital Agency Increases Deal Closes by 35%

How AI-powered lead scoring and automated CRM workflows eliminated lead loss and accelerated sales cycles.

THE CHALLENGE

The Problem: Lost Leads in Translation

The Situation

A B2B digital agency was generating lots of leads but losing them between discovery and sales:

  • ✗ Leads coming from multiple sources (website forms, LinkedIn, Google Ads, Webinars)
  • ✗ Manually checking each lead & deciding who to contact
  • ✗ Leads sitting in inboxes for 2-3 days before reaching sales
  • ✗ Sales team didn't know about high-value prospects
  • ✗ No automated outreach or follow-up

The Business Impact

  • 📉
    Only 20% lead-to-deal conversion rate (industry standard is 35%+)
  • ⏱️
    45+ day sales cycle (should be 20-30 days)
  • 😞
    High-value leads lost to competitors who respond faster
  • 💰
    Millions in revenue left on table each year
THE SOLUTION

AI-Driven Lead Qualification & Instant Routing

The Automated Workflow We Built

1

Multi-Source Lead Aggregation

All leads (forms, ads, LinkedIn, emails) flow into a single intake system

2

AI Lead Qualification

ChatGPT analyzes company size, budget, needs, fit → assigns quality score (1-100)

3

Automatic Lead Routing

Score > 80? Routed to Account Executives. 60-80? To SDRs. Below 60? Nurture sequence.

4

HubSpot + CRM Sync

Lead instantly added to HubSpot with score, source, and recommended action

5

Real-Time Slack Alerts

Sales team gets instant Slack notification with lead preview and suggested next steps

6

Personalized Email Gen

AI generates customized outreach email based on lead profile → saves 5+ min per outreach

Tech Stack

  • 🛠️ Make.com - Workflow automation
  • 🤖 ChatGPT - AI lead scoring
  • 📊 HubSpot - CRM
  • 💬 Slack - Alerts & notifications

Timeline

  • Week 1: Audit lead sources & sales process
  • Week 2: Build automation & train AI model
  • Week 3: Test with new leads, optimize scoring
  • Week 4: Full launch & sales team training

KEY LEARNINGS

What Made This Successful

1. AI Scoring Saved Hours

Manual qualification was 10+ hours/week. AI does it instantly with better accuracy.

2. Speed Matters More Than Perfection

Being 90% right in 5 minutes beats being 100% right in 3 hours. Competitors don't wait.

3. Slack Notifications = Adoption

Push notifications to Slack meant sales team engaged immediately. No login required.

4. Customize AI Prompts

We tuned ChatGPT's scoring criteria to their ideal customer profile. Generic prompts missed key signals.

5. Dashboard for Analytics

Real-time dashboards showing lead source performance, conversion by score, etc. Data-driven decisions.

6. Continuous Refinement

Month 2: Added scoring for vertical fit. Month 3: Added budget predictors. System improves over time.
