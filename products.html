OUR PRODUCTS

CRM, Agent, and Automation Infrastructure

Axxeler builds production-ready customer systems: WhatsApp automation, conversational voice agents, CRM workflows, cross-platform integrations, and developer tooling that connects your revenue stack end to end.

PRODUCT 01

WhatsApp CRM & Automation Platform

A WhatsApp-first CRM that centralizes customer conversations, lead data, sales assignments, broadcast campaigns, and post-sale support in one operational workspace.

  • Customer management: Centralized WhatsApp conversations, customer profiles, interaction history, and automatic lifecycle updates.
  • Automated messaging: Broadcast campaigns, triggered responses, segmented follow-ups, and personalized customer journeys.
  • Lead qualification: Capture leads from WhatsApp, score intent, assign to sales teams, and track conversation status.
  • Analytics: Conversation quality, response time, engagement, conversion rate, and ROI reporting.
PRODUCT 02

Voice Agents

Deployment of LLM-powered conversational IVR systems capable of low-latency, natural language processing. Features include real-time intent recognition and multi-dialect support to automate complex telephonic workflows.

  • Intent routing: Understand caller goals and route to the right workflow, agent, or escalation path.
  • Real-time orchestration: Connect calls to CRM updates, ticket creation, scheduling, reminders, and payment flows.
  • Reliability layer: Fallback scripts, human handoff, call summaries, and audit trails for enterprise operations.
PRODUCT 03

WhatsApp Agent

Enterprise-grade WhatsApp Business API integration for automated customer support. Facilitates rich media interactions, including secure document sharing and real-time location services, within a scalable conversational framework.

  • Support automation: Resolve FAQs, order status, appointment updates, and document requests inside WhatsApp.
  • Rich media flows: Send images, PDFs, location pins, forms, payment links, and verified business templates.
  • Compliance-ready operations: Consent tracking, template governance, handoff rules, and CRM logging.
PRODUCT 04

CRM for All

A flexible CRM foundation for organizations that need clean contact data, consistent sales execution, activity management, reporting, and team collaboration without heavy implementation overhead.

  • Contact intelligence: Segmentation, tags, history, ownership, and 360-degree customer views.
  • Pipeline operations: Custom sales stages, task reminders, lead source attribution, and forecasting.
  • Team workflows: Assignments, shared notes, follow-up tasks, calendar links, and document management.
